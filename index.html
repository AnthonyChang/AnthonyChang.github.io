About Me

I am a full-stack developer with a year and half of development experience gained from working at Symantec Corporation and SAP Canada. My educational background is an advanced diploma in Computer Programming and Analysis from Conestoga College. I also have a Bachelor’s degree in International Business Management. Through my work and school projects, I have gained diverse experience in web development, game development, mobile applications, relational database management systems and software quality assurance. Some of my past work experience has been in management positions for large companies such as Blue Coat Systems, Symantec Corporation, and Aramark Corporation. I am always looking for new projects in the technological space such as cryptocurrencies, 3D-printing, computer hardware, video production, music production, and video games.
